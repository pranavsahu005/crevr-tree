ID: tech_software_jee
Category: Technology → Software Development
Difficulty: High  |  Demand: Very High
Automation Risk: Medium  |  Remote Potential: Very High

DESCRIPTION:
India's most competitive engineering path. JEE → IIT/NIT → Top tech
roles in India and globally.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CAREER STEPS (LINEAR PATH):
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 1: School (Class 6–10)
    → Build strong Maths & Science base. Olympiads (NSO, IMO) are a bonus.

  STEP 2: Class 11–12 (PCM)
    → Join JEE coaching (Allen, Resonance, Aakash or free: Unacademy, PW). Focus: Physics, Chemistry, Maths.

  STEP 3: JEE Main + Advanced
    → JEE Main (NTA) → JEE Advanced (IIT). Score determines IIT/NIT/IIIT/State college.

  STEP 4: B.Tech (4 years)
    → CSE / IT / ECE preferred. Focus on DSA, projects, open source from Year 1.

  STEP 5: Internships (Year 2–4)
    → Target Google, Microsoft, Flipkart, startups. Use LinkedIn, Internshala, AngelList.

  STEP 6: Entry Role (0–2 yrs)
    → Junior Software Engineer / SDE-1. Salary: ₹6–25 LPA depending on company.

  STEP 7: Mid Level (2–5 yrs)
    → SDE-2 / Software Engineer II. Own features, mentor juniors. ₹20–50 LPA.

  STEP 8: Senior (5–8 yrs)
    → Senior SDE / Staff Engineer. System design, architecture ownership. ₹40–1.2 Cr.

  STEP 9: Lead / Principal (8–12 yrs)
    → Tech Lead / Principal Engineer. Cross-team impact. ₹80 L–2 Cr+.

  STEP 10: Management Track
    → Engineering Manager → Director of Engineering → VP Eng → CTO.

  STEP 11: Entrepreneurship
    → Found a startup. Use network from IIT/NIT. Apply to YC, Sequoia Surge, Accel India.

CAREER PATH: DOCTOR (MBBS + SPECIALIZATION)
ID: medical_mbbs
Category: Healthcare → Medical
Difficulty: Very High  |  Demand: Very High
Automation Risk: Very Low  |  Remote Potential: Low

DESCRIPTION:
India's most respected professional path. NEET is the gateway to all
government and private medical colleges.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CAREER STEPS (LINEAR PATH):
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 1: Class 6–10
    → Build strong Biology, Chemistry, Physics base. Score 90%+ in Science.

  STEP 2: Class 11–12 (PCB)
    → Physics, Chemistry, Biology. NEET coaching mandatory. Free resources: Unacademy, PW. Paid: Aakash, Allen.

  STEP 3: NEET UG
    → National Eligibility cum Entrance Test. 720 marks. Government college: 600+ required for good colleges. Held annually by NTA.

  STEP 4: MBBS (5.5 years)
    → 4.5 years academics + 1 year rotating internship. Government colleges: ₹10K–1.5L/yr fees. Private: ₹10–25L/yr.

  STEP 5: MBBS Internship (1 yr)
    → Mandatory clinical rotation across all departments. Stipend: ₹8K–25K/month.

  STEP 6: NEXT / NEET PG (choose specialization)
    → Previously MCI exam, now NEXT. Gives access to MD/MS programs. Rank determines branch and college.

  STEP 7: MD / MS (3 years)
    → Specialization. MD: medicine branches (Dermatology, Psychiatry, Radiology, etc). MS: surgery branches (Ortho, Ophthalmology, ENT, General Surgery).

  STEP 8: Super Specialization — DM / MCh
    → 2–3 years. DM: Cardiology, Nephrology, Neurology. MCh: Cardiac Surgery, Neurosurgery, Plastic Surgery.

  STEP 9: Practice
    → Govt hospital (service + salary), Private hospital (higher pay), Own clinic/nursing home (entrepreneurial).

  STEP 10: Career Peak
    → Senior Consultant / HOD / Professor + HOD / Medical Director / Hospital Chain Owner.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
BRANCH PATHS (ALTERNATE ROUTES FROM THIS CAREER):
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  [From Step 4] Dermatology + Cosmetology
    → MBBS → MD Dermatology → Cosmetic Dermatologist → Own clinic → ₹5–50L/month possible

  [From Step 4] Psychiatry
    → MBBS → MD Psychiatry → Mental health hospitals, private practice → Huge demand post-COVID

  [From Step 4] Radiology / AI in Medicine
    → MBBS → MD Radiology → Tele-radiology (remote, very high pay) → AI Radiology tools collaboration

  [From Step 4] Medical Education
    → MBBS → MD → DNB/Teaching post → Professor → Dean → College Principal

  [From Step 4] Healthcare Administration
    → MBBS → MBA Hospital Management → CEO of hospital chain → Health startup founder

  [From Step 4] Public Health / UPSC
    → MBBS → MD Community Medicine → WHO/UNICEF → IAS (Medical Officer route through UPSC)

  [From Step 7] Cardiology
    → MBBS → MD Medicine → DM Cardiology → Interventional Cardiologist → ₹1–5 Cr/yr

  [From Step 7] Orthopaedics
    → MBBS → MS Orthopaedics → Sports Medicine / Joint Replacement Specialist → High demand

  [From Step 7] Ophthalmology
    → MBBS → MS Ophthalmology → LASIK/Cataract surgeon → Retina specialist → Own eye hospital

ID: agri_career
Category: Agriculture → Farming & AgriTech
Difficulty: Low-Medium  |  Demand: High
Automation Risk: Medium  |  Remote Potential: Low

DESCRIPTION:
India is an agrarian economy. Modern agriculture + AgriTech = massive
opportunity. From farming to agri-startups to government roles.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
CAREER STEPS (LINEAR PATH):
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  STEP 1: Education Options
    → B.Sc Agriculture (4 yrs via ICAR-AIEEA or state exams) OR B.Tech AgriTech/Food Tech OR Diploma in Agriculture (2 yrs, state level).

  STEP 2: Core Knowledge
    → Soil science, Crop science, Agronomy, Plant pathology, Agricultural economics. Hands-on farm experience is essential.

  STEP 3: Career Paths
    → Govt agriculture officer, agri input company, AgriTech startup, farming entrepreneur, cooperative sector, export/import, teaching.

  STEP 4: Government Jobs
    → Agriculture Officer (State PSC) / NABARD Officer / FCI (Food Corporation of India) / IARI Scientist / Agricultural Scientist (ICAR) / Block Agriculture Officer.

  STEP 5: AgriTech Sector
    → Work at agri-startups: drone services, precision farming, soil testing, farmer advisory apps, agri e-commerce.

  STEP 6: Entrepreneurship
    → Organic farming / Poly house / Mushroom cultivation / Apiculture / Dairy / Agro-processing unit — all supported by PM schemes.

  [From Step 2] Agri Business
